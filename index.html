Hydrarch Succession

Succession that starts in water bodies (like ponds) and ends as a land ecosystem (like a forest).

  • Pioneer Stage: Floating plants like algae.
  • Submerged Stage: Underwater plants like hydrilla.
  • Emergent Stage: Grasses and reeds.
  • Climax Stage: Forest ecosystem.

Example: A pond slowly fills with soil, grows grasses and shrubs, and eventually turns into a forest. üåä‚û°Ô∏èüå≥

Xerarch Succession

  • Pioneer Stage: Lichens and mosses.
  • Herb Stage: Grasses and small plants.
  • Climax Stage: Forest ecosystem.
